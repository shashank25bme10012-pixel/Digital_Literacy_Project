# UPI Payment Fraud Case Study

With the rise of digital payments in India, UPI-based fraud has become increasingly common. One typical cybercrime scenario involves scammers tricking users into approving fraudulent payment requests. In this case, a student receives a call from a fraudster pretending to be a bank official or online seller. The scammer claims that money will be credited to the student’s account and asks them to “approve” a request on their UPI app.

## How the Fraud Happens (Step-by-Step)

1. The victim receives a call or message from the scammer.
2. The scammer sends a “collect request” through a UPI app.
3. The victim believes they will receive money.
4. The victim enters their UPI PIN to approve the request.
5. Instead of receiving money, the amount gets debited from their account.
6. In some cases, victims also share OTPs or click malicious links.

## Targeted Victims

- Students
- Elderly people
- Individuals with limited digital awareness

## Consequences

- Financial loss
- Mental stress
- Misuse of personal data
- Difficulty in recovering money

## Conclusion

This case highlights the importance of digital literacy and awareness about safe online payment practices.
