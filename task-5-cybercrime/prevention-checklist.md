# Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips

  Never share your **UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details** with anyone
  Do not approve **unknown collect/payment requests**
  Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be bank officials
  Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown apps
  Use strong passwords and enable **2-factor authentication**
  Regularly check your bank statements for unusual transactions
  Only use trusted and official apps for online payments
  Log out from devices after using financial apps

## UPI Safety (Important)

  Receiving money does **NOT** require a UPI PIN
  Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people
  Double-check payment details before confirming

## Reporting Cybercrime

  National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
  Helpline Number: **1930**
